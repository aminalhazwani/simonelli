text: Flutti was presented during Euroluce 2011.
Flutti is a collection of suspending and wall lamps.
The collection is made by metal sheets fot the italian company Gibas-Fabbrica Artigiana Lampadari, which combines in his production italian craft and new technologies. The collection presents a family of suspending lamps and a family of wall lamps in 3 different sizes. All the lamps are made by calendered metal sheets, sheets are multiple and interchangeable to obtain different color combinations.
